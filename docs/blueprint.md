# **App Name**: BoldNet Digital

## Core Features:

- Client Showcase Slider: An animated slider to showcase client logos with transition effects.
- Services Overview: Display the agency's services: UGC Videos, Website & Landing Page Creation, Professional Post Design, and Ads & Advertising.
- High-Performance Funnel Display: Visualize the agency's funnel (Online Presence, Videos, Advertising, Website & Landing Pages) with descriptions.
- Contact Form: A form to allow potential clients to contact BoldNet Digital for inquiries.

## Style Guidelines:

- Primary color: Bright red (#FF4136) to convey boldness and energy, fitting for a digital marketing agency.
- Background color: Off-white (#F0F0F0) to provide a clean and modern backdrop, ensuring readability and focus on content.
- Accent color: A slightly desaturated red (#D4322A) to complement the primary, used in button hovers or other accents
- Body and headline font: 'Inter', a grotesque sans-serif, for both headlines and body text, giving the website a modern and objective look
- Use simple, flat icons in shades of red to represent each service and funnel step, maintaining a consistent visual language.
- Clean and modern layout with clear sections for clients, services, and funnel steps. Prioritize readability and visual appeal.
- Subtle animations for the client logo slider and section transitions to add a touch of interactivity and visual interest.
